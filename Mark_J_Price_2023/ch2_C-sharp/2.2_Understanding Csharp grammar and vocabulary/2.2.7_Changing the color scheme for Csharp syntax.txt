
In Visual Studio 2022:

    1.   Navigate to "Tools | Options"

    2.  In the "Options" dialog box, in the "Environment" section, select "Fonts and Colors", 
        and then select the display items that you would like to customize. 
        You can also search for the section instead of browsing for it.



In Visual Studio Code:

    1.  Navigate to "File | Preferences | Themes | Color Theme".
        It is in the "Code" menu on macOS.
